Features

Built different

Every detail of PichiPie TV was designed with a premium, cinema-first philosophy.

🎬

Cinematic Splash

Entrance splash screen animation with drifting ambient orbs and a pulsing loader for dynamic network buffering.

🖥️

Dual Platform

Full remote control D-Pad support optimized for Android TV/Firestick, paired with swipe gestures for mobile.

💎

Glassmorphic OSD

On-Screen Display showing channels info, logos, resolution overlays, and active EPG schedules on a dark glass card.

Instantly star streams to compile a custom favorites overlay, with one-tap lists filtering built entirely client-side.
